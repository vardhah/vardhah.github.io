## Overview

Presents the design and simulation platform architecture for evaluating grid distribution systems and transactive energy mechanisms. Published at HotSoS 2019, highlighting the security aspects of transactive energy systems.

## Key Contributions

- Domain-specific modeling environment for grid distribution systems
- Integration with NIST Transactive Energy Challenge component models
- Security-aware simulation capabilities for evaluating resilience of TE mechanisms
